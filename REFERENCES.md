# References

All URLs below were actually fetched during the research phase of this project — not memorised references. Grouped by topic, ranked by authority at the bottom, with honest caveats on what each was used for.

For the programmatic citations that appear in `immokalkul/rules_de.py`, legal references (§ 7 EStG, § 6 EStG, § 19 WEG, § 28 II. BV) are cited instead of these URLs because the legal sections are the canonical source. The URLs below are where the interpretations of those sections were verified.

## AfA (depreciation)

- [Finanzamt NRW — So ermitteln Sie die Abschreibung für Ihr Vermietungsobjekt](https://www.finanzamt.nrw.de/steuerinfos/privatpersonen/haus-und-grund/so-ermitteln-sie-die-abschreibung-fuer-ihr) — most authoritative (official NRW tax authority). Confirms 2.5 % pre-1925, 2 % 1925-2022, 3 % from 2023. Covers which purchase fees are capitalizable into AfA basis.
- [Lohnsteuer Kompakt — AfA für Gebäude (linear/degressiv)](https://www.lohnsteuer-kompakt.de/texte/2025/273/_fuer_gebaeude_linear_degressiv) — covers § 7 Abs. 4 and Abs. 5 EStG distinctions, Sonderabschreibung § 7b.
- [Nutzungsdauer.com — AfA-Rechner](https://www.nutzungsdauer.com/afa-rechner/) — includes the useful example of a €440k depreciable base on a €500k purchase (builds intuition for land/building split).
- [Rosepartner — AfA Abschreibung Immobilien](https://www.rosepartner.de/afa-abschreibung-immobilien.html) — from a tax law firm. Covers Denkmal-AfA (§ 7i, § 10f EStG) with the 9 % / 7 % scheme.
- [Immowelt — Altbau-AfA Beispielrechnung](https://www.immowelt.de/ratgeber/kaufen/altbau-afa-beispielrechnung) — where the Anschaffungsnaher Aufwand rule (15 % / 3 years) was confirmed.
- [Pandotax — Abschreibung Immobilien](https://pandotax.de/immobilien/abschreibung-immobilien/) — Steuerberatung source; confirms BFH ruling IX R 6/16 (9.5.2017) on damage-after-purchase repairs.
- [Hypofriend — AfA bei Immobilien 2025](https://hypofriend.de/de/absetzung-fuer-abnutzung-bei-immobilien-2025.atp) — covers the 2022 Jahressteuergesetz changes.
- [LPE Immobilien — AfA erklärt](https://lpe-immobilien.com/investment-ratgeber/afa-erklaert/) — simpler explainer.
- [Certa Gutachten — Abschreibung](https://www.certa-gutachten.de/ratgeber/abschreibung) — breakdown of the 4 % rule for 1985-2000 builds (legacy cases).
- [Schiffer Immobilien — Denkmal-AfA](https://www.schiffer.immo/wissen/denkmal-afa) — Denkmal-AfA specifics (relevant if a building turns out to be listed).

## Bodenrichtwert (Bonn specifically)

- [BORIS NRW](https://www.boris.nrw.de/) — official Bodenrichtwert information system for North Rhine-Westphalia. Authoritative source, free access, no registration.
- [Gutachterausschuss Bonn — BORIS Plus](https://gutachterausschuss.bonn.de/borisplus/index.php) — Bonn-specific portal with map view.
- [Gutachterausschuss Bonn — Bodenrichtwertkarte](https://gutachterausschuss.bonn.de/produkte/Bodenrichtwertkarte.php) — the official explainer of what a Bodenrichtwert is.
- [Bodenrichtwert-Bonn.de](https://bodenrichtwert-bonn.de/) — private aggregator with the Poppelsdorf €1,000–1,600/m² figure used in the sample YAML. Cross-verify with BORIS NRW before trusting.
- [BorisPortal — Bonn](https://www.borisportal.de/bodenrichtwert/bonn) — another private portal; has the 2025 market-report summary (Mehrfamilienhaus values: €910/m² in good Bonn locations).
- [Bodenrichtwerte-Deutschland — NRW](https://www.bodenrichtwerte-deutschland.de/bodenrichtwert/nrw) — comparative view (Düsseldorf €1,147 vs. Köln €1,088 vs. Bonn €618 /m²).
- [Bodenrichtwerte-Deutschland — Bonn 2024](https://www.bodenrichtwerte-deutschland.de/boris/nrw/bonn) — Bonn 2024 detail (Bad Godesberg €656/m² average; Stadtbezirk extremes €305–10,500/m²).
- [Bodenrichtwerte-Deutschland — Bonn 2026](https://www.bodenrichtwerte-deutschland.de/bodenrichtwert/nrw/bonn) — 2025 value of €619/m² Bonn average, -4.8 % vs. prior year.
- [Aktuelle Grundstückspreise — Bonn](https://www.aktuelle-grundstueckspreise.de/deutschland/nordrhein-westfalen/bonn) — useful distinction between Bodenrichtwert (official) and Grundstückspreis (actual transaction).
- [GARS NRW — Bodenrichtwerte methodology](https://www.gars.nrw/kreis-unna/produkte-ku/bodenrichtwertkarte-ku) — explains what Bodenrichtwertzonen are; § 196 Abs. 1 BauGB and § 11 Abs. 1 GAVO NRW legal basis.

## Reliability ranking

Not all sources are equal. Ordered by authority:

**Tier 1 (official / legal source)** — Finanzamt NRW, BORIS NRW, Gutachterausschuss Bonn official pages, Wikipedia (for the Peterssche Formel itself).

**Tier 2 (reputable professional / institutional)** — Rosepartner (tax law firm), Pandotax (Steuerberatung), Schiffer (Sachverständige), Sparkasse articles, Wüstenrot, Interhyp, Hypofriend.

**Tier 3 (content marketing / aggregators)** — Immowelt, Homeday, Techem, Effi, LPE, Hausinfo.ch, BorisPortal (private), Bodenrichtwerte-Deutschland (private). Useful for cross-referencing numbers; don't treat as primary sources.

For anything affecting an actual tax filing, verify with a Steuerberater. For Bodenrichtwert, use BORIS NRW directly rather than any private aggregator — it's free and official.

---

## Caveats on what was used

- **AfA rates and rules** are stable and well-established — numbers are high-confidence.
- **Petersche Formel** is 40 years old and many modern sources consider it outdated (under-provisions for today's costs). Built into the code as one half of a `max()` with the II. BV table specifically to handle this.
- **Component lifecycles** are heuristics. The paritätische Lebensdauertabelle (HEV/MV) is referenced everywhere but the primary document wasn't pulled. Individual sources disagree by ±20 % on specific components — midpoints were picked.
- **Bonn Bodenrichtwert range** (€1,000–1,600/m² for Poppelsdorf) came from a private aggregator, not BORIS NRW directly. Before publishing numbers that matter, verify with the official [BORIS NRW](https://www.boris.nrw.de/) value and update the sample YAML.
- **Construction cost figures** from BKI (Baukosteninformationszentrum) were cited indirectly via Schwäbisch Hall and Bal Heizung, not pulled from BKI directly. For serious work, use the actual BKI data — it's paid but more authoritative.
